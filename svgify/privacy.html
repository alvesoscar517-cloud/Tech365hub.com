Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2024

Introduction

At SVGify, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

SVGify processes images that you choose to convert. However, all image processing happens locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your images to any external servers.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, non-personal information such as:

  • Extension usage statistics (anonymous)
  • Error reports to improve functionality
  • Browser type and version

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the extension functionality
  • Improve user experience and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Communicate important updates about the extension

Data Storage and Security

SVGify is designed with privacy as a priority:

  • Local Processing: All image conversions happen entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your images or converted files on any servers.
  • Browser Storage: User preferences may be stored locally using Chrome's storage API for your convenience.
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices to protect any data we process.

Third-Party Services

SVGify does not share your data with third parties. We do not use analytics services that track individual users. Any anonymous usage statistics are aggregated and cannot be used to identify you.

Cookies and Tracking

SVGify does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not track your browsing activity outside of the extension's functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use the extension without providing personal information
  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Clear any locally stored preferences through your browser settings
  • Request information about data practices by contacting us

Children's Privacy

SVGify does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general use and does not require age verification. If you believe a child has provided information through our extension, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store your images or personal data on our servers, there is no data retention period. Any preferences stored locally in your browser remain until you clear them or uninstall the extension.

International Users

SVGify can be used globally. Since all processing happens locally in your browser, your data never crosses international borders through our services.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through:

  • Chrome Web Store support page
  • Extension feedback form

Privacy Summary

SVGify is built with privacy at its core. Your images are processed entirely on your device, never uploaded to any server. We don't collect personal information, track your activity, or share data with third parties. You maintain complete control over your data.
